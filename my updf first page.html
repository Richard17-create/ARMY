Introduction

The Uganda People’s Defence Force (UPDF) is the national military of Uganda, tasked with defending the sovereignty, peace, and territorial integrity of the nation. It also participates in peacekeeping missions and supports civil authorities.

Brief History

The UPDF evolved from the National Resistance Army (NRA) after the 1986 liberation struggle was against broader parterns of decitatorship,corruption and instability under leaders like Id-Amin Dada and Milton Obote. In January 1986,the NRA succeed in overthrowing the government this is often called the "liberation of Uganda " the NRA then took power and Yoweri Kaguta Museveni became the president. Today, it is a modern and professional force.

Structure of UPDF

The UPDF is an organisation under the Ministry of defence and Veteran Affairs(MoDVA). The president is the commander in chief of the armed forces.
